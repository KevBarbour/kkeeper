About

Krypto Keeper began as a project for new people entering the CryptoCurrency industry who do not know the major players or understand the history.

Values

Krypto Keeper is dedicated to projecting the unbiased truth about the professional achievement of each of the highlighted members of the community.

Company Goal

-The primary goal of Krypto Keeper is so a newbie can quickly and easily look into the profiles of each person listed on the site and gather a fundamental understanding of who to follow. Understanding the key players involved in any major movement is an essential component for the prediction of its success or failure.

-The secondary goal is to help recognise the members of the community for professional achievement in a groundbreaking industry.
